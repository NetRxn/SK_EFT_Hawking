## Why 5y's closure doesn't propagate

Phase 5u formalizes analog Hawking radiation in polariton superfluids:
- **Sonic horizon** in a polariton condensate — Layer-2 EFT of the
  Schwinger-Keldysh type
- **Dissipative corrections** `δ_disp, δ_diss, δ_cross` to the
  Hawking temperature
- **Laboratory shot-count predictions** for the Paris LKB apparatus

None of these touch:
- Cosmological-scale emergent vacuum (5y subject)
- Gibbs-Duhem obstruction (dark-energy-specific)
- DESI DR2 phenomenology (cosmology, not lab physics)

The 5u experimental regime is `T_H ∼ 1 K`, ~10¹⁰× hotter than
cosmological scales. Entirely different physics.
