## Headline: NO inheritance

**Phase 5x does not inherit Phase 5y's NO-GO verdict.** Dark matter at
EFT-agnostic level (5x target) rests on fundamentally different
mechanisms from the late-time dark-energy frameworks evaluated in 5y.
The obstruction landscape is different, the relevant physics scales
are different, and the experimental handles are different.

## Why 5y's closure doesn't propagate

Phase 5y's Gibbs-Duhem obstruction applies to:
- **Single-scalar self-tuning** emergent-vacuum frameworks
- With **standard emergent-vacuum action** `L = R/(16πG) + ε(q) + L_SM`
- Under **Gibbs-Duhem equilibrium** `ρ_V(q₀) = 0, dρ_V/dq|_{q₀} = 0`

The obstruction is about `w_vac = −1` lock + DESI-incompatibility, a
**late-time cosmological** concern. Dark matter is **sub-Hubble-scale**,
non-relativistic, and typically couples gravitationally only — none of
the three hypotheses above apply.
