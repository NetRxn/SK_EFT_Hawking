## 0. Motivation

We have every piece EXCEPT the TQFT functor itself. TQFTPartition.lean computes Z(Σ_g) from MTC data, but this is the OUTPUT of the functor, not the functor. The WRT construction assigns:
- To a surface Σ: a vector space V(Σ)
- To a cobordism M: Σ_in → Σ_out: a linear map V(Σ_in) → V(Σ_out)

This would be the **first complete verified TQFT in any proof assistant** and completes the chain from lattice integrable systems to gauge theory.
