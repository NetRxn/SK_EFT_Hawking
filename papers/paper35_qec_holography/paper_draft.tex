\begin{abstract}
We formalize the Hayden--Preskill (2007, arXiv:0708.4025)
information-recovery protocol on the modular-tensor-category horizon
substrate established in Phase 6a Wave 3
(\verb|BHEntropyMicroscopic.lean|).  Two structural observables ship
on top of the existing \verb|HorizonMTCBC| anchor: a code-distance
proxy $\dC := \log\dmax$, identical to the W3 area-law leading
coefficient $\kappa_C$, and a Hayden--Preskill scrambling-time bound
$\Tscr := \log\Dsq = \log\sum_a d_a^2$, the substrate-only part of
$(\beta/2\pi) S_{\mathrm{BH}}$ after the area law is pulled out.  The
wave's named correctness-push,
\verb|code_distance_scaling_matches_anyonic_fusion_iff_fusion_in_admissible_class|,
proves a load-bearing biconditional plus a forward implication:
$\dC > 0$ iff $\dmax > 1$ (admissibility = non-abelian fusion class),
and admissibility forces $\Tscr > 0$ via $\Dsq \ge \dmax^2$.  The
Fibonacci substrate is the concrete witness ($\dC = \log\varphi
\approx 0.481 < \log 2$), with the W3 theorem
\verb|fibonacci_horizon_areaLawKappa_pos| invoked via the
correctness-push to ship
\verb|fibonacci_HPCode_scramblingTimeBound_pos|.  The trivial-abelian
MTC ($\dmax = 1$) ships as the falsifier
\verb|trivialAbelian_violates_admissibility|.  A substantive
cross-bridge to the W3 horizon-BC bundle,
\verb|horizon_BC_implies_HP_admissible|, invokes
$h.\mathtt{areaLeading}$ (the W3 field asserting $0 < \log\dmax$) to
forward $H_{\mathrm{HorizonBoundaryCondition}}$ to W4 admissibility.
The module ships $10$ substantive Lean theorems with $0$ \verb|sorry|
and $0$ new axioms, a Python mirror with $30$ pytest cross-checks,
and a numerical figure spanning Fibonacci, Ising, SU(3)$_{k=2}$
Fibonacci sub-sector, and trivial-abelian substrates.  The
AdS/CFT spectrum identification and explicit Yoshida--Kitaev decoder
construction are deferred per the Phase 6c roadmap \S A scope-tightening
for W4.
\end{abstract}

\section{Motivation: structural QEC on the horizon MTC substrate}
\label{sec:motivation}

The Phase 6a Wave 3 module \verb|BHEntropyMicroscopic.lean| ships the
\verb|HorizonMTCBC| structure as the abstract MTC-categorical horizon
boundary condition for emergent gravity: a finite simple-object count,
per-object positive quantum dimensions, a unit object with $d=1$, an
attainable maximum $\dmax$, and the Immirzi tuning parameter
$\gamma$.  Two derived observables ship downstream: the global
dimension squared $\Dsq = \sum_a d_a^2$ (W3
\verb|globalDimSq|) and the area-law leading coefficient
$\kappa_C := \log\dmax$ (W3 \verb|areaLawKappa|).

This wave reuses both observables to formalize the Hayden--Preskill
holographic-QEC content on the same substrate.  No new physical
quantities are introduced; the W4 contribution is the
\emph{interpretation} of $\kappa_C$ as a code-distance proxy and
$\log\Dsq$ as a scrambling-time lower bound, plus the
biconditional connection to non-abelian admissibility.

\label{sec:dC_tscr}

\section{Hayden--Preskill recovery}
\label{sec:recovery}

The structural recovery condition ships as the predicate
\begin{verbatim}
def recoveryPossible
    (H : HPCode) (B : R) : Prop :=
  Real.log (H.horizon.quantum_dim
            H.encoding_obj) <= B
\end{verbatim}
where $B$ is the boundary-radiation entropy.  The substantive
content is the Page-time-like threshold
$B \ge \log d_{\mathrm{encode}}$ as the necessary entropy for
recovery on the MTC substrate.  The theorem
\verb|recovery_at_scrambling_bound| proves recovery is information-
theoretically possible at the scrambling-time bound for \emph{any}
encoding choice: by case-splitting on $d_{\mathrm{encode}} \ge 1$
versus $d_{\mathrm{encode}} < 1$, both branches yield
$\log d_{\mathrm{encode}} \le \log\Dsq$.  The first uses
$d_{\mathrm{encode}} \le d_{\mathrm{encode}}^2 \le \Dsq$
(via $1 \le d_{\mathrm{encode}}$ and the sum bound); the second
uses $\log d_{\mathrm{encode}} \le 0 \le \log\Dsq$.
