infrastructure currently has multiple paths for the same paper to be
"verified" while pointing at materially different bibliographic facts.

**Proposed QI item: qi-bibitem-title-strict-match.** Extend
`validate.py --check citation_primary_sources_present` to a *strict mode*
that fetches the cached primary-source PDF, extracts the title via pdfminer
(per the Session-5 primary-source verification policy in
`feedback_primary_source_webfetch_policy.md`), and compares to the
bibitem's title field. Flag titles that differ by >2 edit distance (catches
typos and capitalization but is not derailed by paraphrasing). Run at every
Stage 13. Owner: pipeline; target date: Phase 7c.

This QI would have caught all six wrong-title bibitems in D2 mechanically.
