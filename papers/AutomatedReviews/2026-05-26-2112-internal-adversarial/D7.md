## QI Candidate

**qi-gate-5-self-audit-blind-spot-on-identity-and-trivial-constructor-bodies.**
The Phase 6w self-conducted audit explicitly applied the 5-question
P3/P5 checklist and correctly remediated T2 of the
AnalogHawkingDemarcation module (`analog_hawking_simulable_iff_loopRate_ok_and_c1_zero`

when the auditor catches one P5 in a module they tend to stop
looking, even when adjacent theorems share the same defect class.
Recommend tightening the self-audit checklist: when one P5 is
caught in a module, the auditor MUST inspect every other theorem
that returns a hypothesis directly OR uses `⟨_, _⟩` / `And.intro` /
trivial-constructor bodies on the same definitional target, before
clearing the module GREEN. The pattern "sibling tautologies of one
remediated tautology" is a high-yield audit target.
