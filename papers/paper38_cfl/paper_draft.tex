\section{CFL chiral Lagrangian skeleton}
\label{sec:lagrangian}

The Lagrangian sector ships at the structural level only — the
canonical kinetic term and the leading mass term:
\begin{align}

    \|\partial \Phi\|^2, \\

\end{align}

  $m_q > 0$ AND $\verb|isCFLPhase|\,\Phi$ — the proof uses the
  qualitative-quantitative bridge to convert $\Phi \neq 0$ to
  $\|\Phi\| > 0$.
\end{itemize}
